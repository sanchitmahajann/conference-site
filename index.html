About

The Department of Networking and Communications, School of Computing of SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Chennai, India is conducting the second edition of the Conference on Advances in Communication Networks and Systems (CoaCoNS) from 26 to 27 March 2025.

Explore cutting-edge developments in communication networks and systems at our pioneering conference. Leading experts gather to share research breakthroughs, innovative solutions, and visionary insights driving this rapidly evolving field forward.
